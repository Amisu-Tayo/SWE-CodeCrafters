PLANT DISEASES

plant disease, an impairment of the normal state of a plant that interrupts or modifies its vital functions.
All species of plants, wild and cultivated alike, are subject to disease. Although each species is susceptible to
characteristic diseases,these are, in each case, relatively few in number. The occurrence and prevalence of
plant diseases vary from season to season, depending on the presence of the pathogen, environmental
conditions, and the crops and varieties grown. Some plant varieties are particularly subject to outbreaks
of diseases while others are more resistant to them. See also list of plant diseases.
